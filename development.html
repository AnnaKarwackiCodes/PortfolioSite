Don't Spend Time On It

Platform: Windows
Unity 5.6.3/C#

Designed with the theme of "Everyday Hero". Two person team, roles on project was co-designer and sole developer.

Made during RIT's Hero Jam Fall 2017 and awarded Best Game.

Inspired by the games: Diner Dash and Typing of the Dead, Don't Spend Time On It is a two player game where one player is playing as a professor and the other is playing as the TA. The professor's goal is to type the words that appear on the board behind them, the TA's job is to run around the classroom and gather as many words as they can for the professor to type. Putting more of the same word up on the board awards more points.

Project Cyber B

Platform: Windows/HP Mixed Reality Headset
Unity 2017.3.1p3/C#

Six person team. Responsibilities on project include: project management, development lead, and VR implementation.

Was displayed at RIT's annual Imagine RIT.

The player is "entering the system" when they put up the headset. The player enters and are trying to get the information in the center on the map and get out. The system is actively trying to remove the player. By sending out waves of enemies to stop the player, they have to use their abilities to fight off the waves of enemies and reach the exit with the information in hand.
