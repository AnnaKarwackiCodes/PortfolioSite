University Of Rochester Medical Game

Adobe Photoshop

This was a game created in a partnership with RIT and the University of Rochester medical department.

Purpose of the game is to help medical students with learning their material. The focus of the game was about pregnancy and all the factors that affect pregnancy.

I was in charge of designing the UI and creating the art assets for the game. When it came to creating the UI I have to keep in mind that the player would not be traditional gamers so I had to design with them in mind.

Null Sector - Tabletop RPG

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Design and developed by a team of 5 people. Main role on project was co-designer of mechanics and visual designer of the system's rulebook and character sheets.

Publish on DrivethruRPG.

In this TTRPG you play in the new USA, under the guidance and watchful eye of an all knowing AI: BINF. The players are all apart of different groups around the capital of this new United States who all have different skill focuses and relationships with the other groups.This system also features a unique reverse dice system where rolling low is your key to success.

This game is focused toward experienced tabletop RPG players.
